# Parachute — 2-Minute Video Pitch Script

**Target: ~2 minutes at natural speaking pace (~280-300 words)**

---

## Script

I'm Aaron Gabriel Neyer, and I'm building Parachute.

Right now, everyone is building personal AI computers. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google — they're all racing to create AI that doesn't just answer questions, but takes action for you. Over a hundred million people are already paying for AI monthly. This is a $50 billion market by 2030.

But here's the problem: they're all building for the 5% — the power users, the developers, the people who are already deep in this world. The other 95% — the artist, the small business owner, the parent who just wants a better way to think and remember — they have no way in.

That's what Parachute is. We're building two products that form one journey.

**Parachute Daily** is a voice-first journal. You talk into a pendant or your phone — on a walk, in the car, wherever you think. Your words get transcribed, organized, and gently enhanced with AI reflections over time. No learning curve. Just talk.

**Parachute Computer** is the full platform underneath — open source, local-first, with a knowledge graph that connects everything you've ever told it.

The key insight is that **context compounds**. Every day you journal, your AI gets meaningfully better. After months of use, it already knows how you think. No competitor can shortcut that. And when you're ready for more, you upgrade — and your brain comes with you.

We're open source, AGPL-3.0 licensed, and structured as a Public Benefit Corporation. Because when an AI knows your most personal thoughts, you need to trust it completely.

We're self-funded. We have a working app, a pendant prototype — I'm wearing one right now — and our beta launches this month.

We're raising $300,000 to bring the core team full-time and launch by June. The future of personal AI should have balance and choice built in from the start. That's Parachute.

---

**Word count: ~285 words (~2 minutes at conversational pace)**
